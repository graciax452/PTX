Our Core Values Drive Our Mission Forward

Honoring Diverse Traditions

Cultural Sensitivity

At Sequoia, we embrace cultural sensitivity, ensuring our services honor diverse backgrounds.We are committed to respecting and honoring the cultural practices of the Indigenous and other communities we serve. Our staff is trained to work in a manner that is both respectful and accommodating.

Value Two

Compassionate Care

Empathy and understanding are at the core of our services. Whether it’s cleaning a home or providing personal care, we approach every task with kindness and a deep commitment to helping our clients feel comfortable.

Value Three

Environmental Responsibility

We care about the planet as much as we care about the people in it. That’s why we use eco-friendly cleaning products and work to minimize waste in all our services.

Empowerment

We believe in empowering our team members and the communities we serve by providing job opportunities, skill development, and respectful, supportive service.
